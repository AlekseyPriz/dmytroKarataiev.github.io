Connect Four


Connect Four (also known as Captain's Mistress, Four Up, Plot Four, Find Four, Fourplay, Four in a Row and Four in a Line) is a two-player connection game in which the players first choose a color and then take turns dropping colored discs from the top into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the next available space within the column. The objective of the game is to connect four of one's own discs of the same color next to each other vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before your opponent. Connect Four is a strongly solved game. Wikipedia

In my implementation of Connect Four you can play against a simple bot who tries to calculate the best move one step further or vs a random bot. You can also play against your real friend.

  • Date: November 2015

Popular Movies


Popular Movies application is a nice, fast and simple client for The Movies Database. During the development of the app I learned a number of extremely important Android technics and technologies.

The most important were:
Content Providers, Underlying Database, Database helpers.
AsyncTasks, Data fetching on background thread.
RecyclerView, RecyclerAdapter.
Separate designs for tablets and phones (Activity vs Fragment implementation).
App uses The Movie Database API to fetch data and show movies

Functionality: You can sort data by: rating, popularity, number of votes, revenue and name in ascending and descending orders. You can easily share info about chosen movie. Scrolling is continuous. You can watch trailers inside of the app (YouTube API). You can save your favorite movies. Search by movie titles. Tablet Design.


  • Google Play
  • Github
  • Client: Personal Project
  • Date: December 2015

Earthquake Survival


Earthquake Survival allows you to see all the earthquakes around the world and notifies about closest to you (you can set the notifying threshold for strength and distance from you). You can find the latest news about earthquakes, share info and statistics with your friends. Earthquake Survival also has a lot of useful information about what to do in case of emergency, who to call, what to buy and how to survive.
